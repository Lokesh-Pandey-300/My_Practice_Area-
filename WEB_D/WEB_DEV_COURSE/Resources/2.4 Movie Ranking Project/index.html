Best Movies According to Lokesh

My Recent Favourities

Bullet Train

"Bullet Train" is an action-packed thriller film directed by David Leitch, based on the novel "Maria Beetle" by Kotaro Isaka. Set aboard a high-speed train in Japan, the story follows a diverse group of assassins with conflicting objectives who find themselves on the same train. As the journey progresses, tensions rise, secrets are revealed, and chaos ensues, leading to a thrilling ride filled with twists and turns. With a stellar cast including Brad Pitt, Sandra Bullock, and Joey King, "Bullet Train" promises adrenaline-fueled excitement and suspense

12th fail

"12th Fail" starring Vikrant Massey is a Bollywood comedy-drama film that follows the story of a young man who faces the setback of failing his 12th-grade exams. Played by Vikrant Massey, the protagonist navigates through the challenges and societal pressures that come with academic failure. As he confronts his own insecurities and battles against stereotypes, he embarks on a journey of self-discovery and personal growth. With humor, heart, and a message of resilience, "12th Fail" offers an inspiring tale about overcoming setbacks and finding one's path in life

Bajirao Mastani

"Bajirao Mastani" is an epic historical romance film directed by Sanjay Leela Bhansali. Set in the early 18th century, it tells the story of the legendary Maratha warrior, Peshwa Bajirao I, and his passionate love affair with Mastani, a warrior princess. The film explores the complexities of their relationship amidst political intrigue, cultural clashes, and societal opposition. With breathtaking visuals, mesmerizing music, and powerful performances by Ranveer Singh, Deepika Padukone, and Priyanka Chopra in the lead roles, "Bajirao Mastani" is a grand cinematic experience that captures the essence of love, sacrifice, and valor in the backdrop of historical India.
